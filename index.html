DEPARTMENT OF TRAINING AND PLACEMENT

Our Vision
Our Mission

The Placement and Training Cell aims to:

• Act as a facilitator to nurture, train, groom and equip our students with the knowledge, technical, personal skill sets and humility to be the select choice of recruiting companies.
• Groom students keeping the vision of the college in mind - to successfully engineer careers and lives of our students with character and care.
• Launch industry-ready students with the knowledge, skill sets and humility for success.

The Placement and Training Cell's mission is to:

• Individually counsel every new student joining this Institute to help them understand their own potential and prepare them to overcome obstacles they face academically or personally.
• Train students in technical concepts for knowledge and skill set building.
• Groom students personalities through soft skills to influence values and belief systems to lead positive attitudinal changes.
• Equip students to handle the challenges of life through life skills coaching.

Placement Team

The Placement Cell of SMVITM has always strived to facilitate placement for its students through three areas Career guidance, Pre-placement training, and Employment/Placement opportunities. Career guidance is given to the students through invited talks from people from various industries giving them an understanding of various sectors and industries that they have the scope in. They are also given orientations on preparations for competitive exams like GATE, GMAT, CAT, TOEFL through various coaching institutions, virtual resources, newsletters, etc.

The College has signed MoUs with various training partners to skill-up the students through Technical, Softskills, and Aptitude training to complement the knowledge gained from stream-related subjects to meet the demands and requirements from industries for the hiring process.

Students have opportunities to apply for recruitment drives of Mass recruiters like Infosys, TCS, Wipro, Cognizant, etc along with mid-sized companies and start-ups across India. Being a VTU affiliated college, we are also supported by the VTU Centralized Placement Cell, through various learning portals, internship & job portals; assessment and training companies. Opportunities to find employment with local companies like Robosoft Technologies, Manipal Technologies, Niveus Solutions, Karmic Designs, etc, are also encouraged.
